Game Controls

Movement Controls

  • W Move Up
  • A Move Left
  • S Move Down
  • D Move Right

Game Mechanics

  • Auto Automatically attack nearby monsters
  • Gems Collect experience gems to level up
  • Level Choose new abilities when leveling up
  • Goal Survive as long as possible!

Pro Tip: Use WASD or Arrow keys to move your character. Keep moving to avoid getting surrounded by monsters!

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival game where you must fight against endless waves of monsters. Control your character and survive as long as possible!

With multiple characters to choose from, various weapons and abilities, and procedurally generated levels, each playthrough offers a unique experience. Collect experience gems, unlock new abilities, and discover the best strategies to survive as long as possible in this addictive game.

Gameplay Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from multiple unique characters, each with their own strengths and abilities. Find the one that best matches your playstyle!

Weapons & Abilities

Discover various weapons and abilities to enhance your character. Combine them strategically to create powerful builds.

Procedural Levels

Experience procedurally generated levels that ensure each playthrough is unique and challenging.

High Score System

Compete for the highest score as you face increasing difficulty over time. Can you become the ultimate survivor?

Survival Tips

  • •

    Keep Moving: Keep moving to avoid getting surrounded by monsters.

  • •

    Collect Experience: Collect experience gems to grow stronger and level up faster.

  • •

    Choose Abilities Wisely: Choose complementary abilities that work well together for maximum effectiveness.

  • •

    Watch Your Health: Keep an eye on your health bar to avoid unexpected defeats.

  • •

    Try Different Builds: Experiment with different character builds to find the most effective strategies.
